Deep Learning Research in Vision-Language Modeling for Compuational Patology

We are pleased to announce open positions for PhD students to engage in cutting edge deep learning research. The primary focus of this research is to develop Vision-language Models (VLMs) in deep learning for automating medical reports from gigapixel Whole Slide Images (WSIs) in clinical pathology. This initiative is part of highly cross-disciplinary research project, bringing together Computer Vision (CV) and Natual Language Processing (NLP) experts in deep learning as well as clinical pathologist for developing cutting edge solutions in computational pathology.
