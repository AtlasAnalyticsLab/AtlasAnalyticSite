Three Graduate Students Secure Over $73,000 in Funding From FRQNT Scholarships

I am very happy to announce that three of my graduate students from the Atlas Analytics Lab have been awarded prestigious scholarships from Fonds de recherche du Québec-FRQNT! Join me in hearty congratulations to Cassandre Notton, Damien Martins Gomes, and Ali Nasiri-Sarvi for their remarkable achievement in securing over $73,000 CAD collectively for their graduate studies in CSSE at Concordia University.

Mahdi S. Hosseini Receives an Amazon Research Award

I’m excited to share that I’ve received an Amazon Research Award for my proposal “Toward Auto-Populating Synoptic Reports in Diagnostic Pathology,” at Concordia University. Thank you AmazonScience for this prestigious recognition that will help me expand my research lab.

Vim4Path: Self-Supervised Vision Mamba for Histopathology Images Accepted in CVPR 2024

Exciting News in Computational Pathology! Our latest research paper on Self-Supervised Learning (SSL) from Gigapixel Whole Slide Images (WSI) has been accepted in CVPR 2024, the 9th Workshop on Computer Vision for Microscopy Image Analysis.
